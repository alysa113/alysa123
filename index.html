Welcome to The Outfit Alley

Growing up in a Nepalese family, Alysa was always moved by the art and culture of Nepal and the neighbouring country India.
Alysa wanted to turn her hobby into a side-business.
Outfit Alley is a new clothing brand decidated in designing south-asian inspired ethnic clothes as well as custom designed outfits.
Alysa is currently based in Brampton, Ontario and is taking orders from customers in GTA.

Design Inspirations

Alysa's design inspirations are the famous hollywood designer Prabal Gurung, our local Mani Jassal, Peachproject by Ayesha, Falguni Shane.
